Can you spot AI output?

The current edition of AI / LLM can be used to build web presence and strengthen brands. However they make distinctive output, I limit to English in this article. So if you are not using AI / LLM 1 2 3, it is advisable to avoid:

  • “Delve”
  • “Explore”
  • “Tapestry”
  • “Testament”
  • “Leverage”
  • “Showcasing”
  • “Underscores”
  • “Potential” +4.1%
  • “Findings” + 2.7%
  • “Crucial” + 2.6%
  • “it is important to note”
  • “as an AI language model” (probably easy here)

Old “AI” products

These haven't been marketed as “an AI product”, but they use the same algorithms as “AI products”. The pursuit of a better product is not today's innovation, “Better mouse traps” 5 that I noticed ::

  • Siri by Apple inc
  • Bayesian email filters
  • Writing apps like Grammarly, Hemmingway, or ProWritingAid (the alternative suggestions generation)
  • Most camera in phones distort the images to make selfies “look better” - this makes skinny people look weird.
  • Google Translate from 2017 (I don't know how other translation tools are made, but there are several)
  • Spotify auto play-list feature
  • Google map route finder feature ~ when they gave it more CPU time in the earlier editions.
  • Some features of Duolingo, used to generate the practice content. The practice sessions are completely tailored to the users writing style, I assume pulled from messaging histories, or spell check history.

At a startup I worked for in early 2015, my last deliverable was to organise a process for a “AI best pricing” feature ~ focussed on ML more than LLM. The market and startups haven't changed, just the hype-machine got much louder.

Structures

I list many specific terms to make the rest of the group of articles easier to read. For words to be useful, they should be orthogonal and well organised. AI currently is so very much more of an advertisement space than other tech 6, and access to terms helps structure and deflate 3rd party data. Note, much of this is stats or words to describe stats. I relegate the Maths proofs and actual equations to the external academic papers etc, as my highest level of detail would be source code ~ as an engineer I write better code, and Maths-first people write better maths. Browsing the terminology shows what LLM authors and researchers needed to describe, and so how LLM are built.

An LLM is structured like a Tensorflow, please browse 7.

This group of articles is mostly focused on how to build and manage a new LLM, but I think some theory will make the terminology and structures easier to understand.
