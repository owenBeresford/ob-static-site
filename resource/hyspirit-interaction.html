I will write about the work I did; rather than what HySpirit is, which is much more interesting. Hyspirit is a logic engine and content retrieval tool, which could be extended into a search engine. The logic aspect is that it understands 4 logical states (true, false, both, and neither), which is better suited to more-realistic and messy data. The least search engine aspect of Hyspirit was the corpus that users searched. My dissertation can be available, mail me if you would like to read it. By now my work is old ~ from 2001 ~ and demonstrates little useful; as I am a much more experienced developer. By my current backend coding work, this was a limited-feature web interface. I left the source at University, as it was their property.
