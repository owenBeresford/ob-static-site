Bootiful Bootick loungery [1]

This maquette exists as all of my partner's underwear seems to have thin elastic (5mm), and all mine is thick width (25mm); as though there is only two choices. I was recently in the Netherlands and walked past a shop called "Lingerie Boetick". This is a creative collision of these two concepts.

After a Nederlandse hardcore rave for 12h, our train home next day was boring and felt sooo slow. I had to do something. There was a weather alert, so the cruising speed was reduced. ... So, this maquette happened, and much laughter.

There are only two choices. Try to get punters to buy as many as possible.

The entire range of options is supported by manufacturing two colours; and two cuts in the necessary sizes. As each product is different you can set a price separately. Since these are different products, you can carry appropriate sizes in each product (i.e. no need to have large size waifs, as that would ruin the brand).
