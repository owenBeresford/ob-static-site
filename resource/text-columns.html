Factors affecting readability of a text:

  • its Fogg metric (ie use of words)
  • colour contrast of text
  • edge of text margins ~ to allow faster detection on edge of text with edge vision
  • the text justification
  • line height, the measure of space around the actual letters
  • line length 13
  • text orientation vs expectations of reader
