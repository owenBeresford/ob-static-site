I offer a choice for fonts, focussing on readability. This is a stupid and simple demo, so you can see them and compare. I see texts saying blah font is specially made to be readable, but none of them are comparing readable fonts.
This is a part of a range of “visual samples / maquettes”. Since I started doing more front-end work, I have been making these to speed up browser testing. #boring I'm starting to think I should add a snoring backtrack to these pages.
If you are using this article to choose a font for use in other sites, please review with both a mac, a phone and pc, as fonts look different on different operating systems. For more notes on readable fonts

I find the first option OK, but any of the other options gives better readability. I note the custom fonts are all a bit smaller than the system one.

Reference:: inline text contains samples of “Annabel Lee” by Edgar Allen Poe docs

List of fonts

The following text is changing font-size to see how small each font can be taken.

  • browser default: large, sample text for letter shapes, Il|17, jl, 96, O0o, bp, 8B
  • default, normal, sentence: It was many and many a year ago, \ In a kingdom by the sea,
  • default, small, sentence: That a maiden there lived whom you may know \ By the name of Annabel Lee;
  • readable 1: large, sample text for letter shapes, Il|17, jl, 96, O0o, bp, 8B
  • readable 1, normal, sentence: And this maiden she lived with no other thought \ Than to love and be loved by me.
  • readable 1, small, sentence: I was a child and she was a child, \ In this kingdom by the sea,
  • readable 2: large, sample text for letter shapes, Il|17, jl, 96, O0o, bp, 8B
  • readable 2: normal, sentence: But we loved with a love that was more than love — \ I and my Annabel Lee—
  • readable 2: small, sentence: With a love that the wingèd seraphs of Heaven \ Coveted her and me.
  • readable 3: large sample text for letter shapes, Il|17, jl, 96, O0o, bp, 8B
  • readable 3, normal, sentence: And this was the reason that, long ago, \ In this kingdom by the sea,
  • readable 3: small, sentence: A wind blew out of a cloud, chilling \ My beautiful Annabel Lee;
  • readable 4: large, sample text for letter shapes, Il|17, jl, 96, O0o, bp, 8B
  • readable 4, normal sentence: So that her highborn kinsmen came \ And bore her away from me,
  • readable 4: small, sentence: To shut her up in a sepulchre \ In this kingdom by the sea.

Scaling fonts by weight

This following text is all in the same size font (i.e. font-size:13px on my machine ), but it doesn't look the same size like this.

  • browser default, light: The angels, not half so happy in Heaven, \ Went envying her and me—
  • default, medium: Yes!— that was the reason (as all men know, \ In this kingdom by the sea)
  • default, heavy: That the wind came out of the cloud by night, \ Chilling and killing my Annabel Lee.
  • readable 1, light: But our love it was stronger by far than the love \ Of those who were older than we—
  • readable 1, medium: Of many far wiser than we— \ And neither the angels in Heaven above
  • readable 1, heavy: Nor the demons down under the sea \ Can ever dissever my soul from the soul
  • readable 2, light: Of the beautiful Annabel Lee; \ For the moon never beams, without bringing me dreams
