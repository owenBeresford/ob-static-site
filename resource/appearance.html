This resource is to control the look and feel for your current session. To be honest, this is to implement, rather than claim righteousness on; making sites easy to use for differing people. I just hope people spot it and use it.
I have been very thorough on listing minor things, that do occur; but everyone must already have an adaption strategy.

If I was starting this site now, I would solve this problem by with third party library e.g. userway. Adaption tooling, beyond static analyse for good practice, was not a known thing in the EU in 2009. I built a entirely local creation, with dynamic CSS, so adding a third party tool would be more fiddly.

NOTE: Applying solutions that mention browser settings will improve your experience in many sites.
